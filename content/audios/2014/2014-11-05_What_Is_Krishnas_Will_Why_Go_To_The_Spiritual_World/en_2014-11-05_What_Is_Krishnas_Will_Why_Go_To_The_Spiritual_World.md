Content:
- Even a blade of grass can not move without Krishna's will and so — what is Krishna's will? Is it revealed to us by everyone around or just by the devotees? And does it mean that whatever they do is already sanctioned by the Lord?

- Our material world is not so perfect and beautiful. But if we serve Krishna and He is everywhere, does it mean that you can live here also — just with your eyes opened? Why do we need to go back to Godhead, to the spiritual world?
