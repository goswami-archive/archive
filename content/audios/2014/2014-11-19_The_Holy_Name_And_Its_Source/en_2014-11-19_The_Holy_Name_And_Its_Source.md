A question about transcendental sound vibration — being more subtle, more reliable and how spiritual life is received: how Krishna consciousness can be more accurately received? — The descent of the Holy Name that Srila Sridhar Maharaj spoke about. — Baladev, Yoga Maya and Srimati Radharani — how are they connected to each other? Their functions in the spiritual world seem to be similar — what is their uniqueness? — The Holy Name descends from above, means — from a superior Vaishnava, so there is no democracy. But in our current situation, the source may be not so obvious. Must there be just one absolute acharya and the others are relative? How can there be several acharyas and what's the ultimate spiritual guidance?
