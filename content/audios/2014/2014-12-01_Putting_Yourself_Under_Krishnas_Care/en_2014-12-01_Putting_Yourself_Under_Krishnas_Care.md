Content:
When do we know that it is the right time to let go and let Krishna look after us? When we no longer have to think about our own well-being, maintenance or protection? In the neophyte position, is it a possibility or would it be delusional or dangerous? — All risk — no gain. — How about that? — Devotees is Krishna's mercy. — Putting yourself under Krishna's care: when is it just the neglection?
