Content:
Is there real happiness in this fighting-and-progressing process? Also, there are some people who just want to dissolve in Brahman, become peaceful with no fight. Why do they have this desire and where is it originally from? — As human beings we have a false ego thinking: “I'm a beautiful lady or even a demigod.” But do animals understand who they are? And how come that something based in ourselves, became our evil guardian? — Krishna consciousness is a descending process, but there are so many stages and types of sadhana which help us to progress from our side, to ascend — but knowing, really, in our hearts that it should come down to us.
