Content:
There are many spiritual books available, some are Mayavadi's and some are Sahajiya's. But new devotees do not have intelligence to tell that. At the same time they don't read our own books because it might be too hard to understand. So how should we approach these devotees and help them? — The day after Govinda Maharaj took sannyas Sridhar Maharaj said that Govinda Maharaj didn't meet Saraswati Thakur, but he has such a deep theistic understanding that it seems as if they met. What does that mean? — In Bhagavad-gita, 4 reasons for serving to the Lord are mentioned: fear, prospective, duty and the highest — out of love. Why is the sense of duty is higher than even the prospect of getting something and precedes the love? Where does this feeling come from?
