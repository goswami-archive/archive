Look, even in this world when you deal with romantic love, people will meet someone and then they’ll say, “I have always and only loved you.” They’ll say, “You mean … did you say that to your ex-girlfriend?”...
Everything is focused on the object of love, the beloved … In Kṛṣṇa conception we are dealing with the compelling, irresistible, all-attractive beauty, seductive charm, sweetness, of Kṛṣṇa …
