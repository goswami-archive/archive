First of all, we should wonder why is it that we’re repeatedly expressing these things? In songs, in poetry, in novels, in motion picture movie, everything, we revisit these subject matters repeatedly. People find themselves in a position where they’re expressing eternal love to someone, when we know, statistically, objectively speaking, it’s not true. 
“I love you forever, or until you’re 40.” There are many different objective criteria that somehow destroy, dissolve, that love, and we’re still hankering for this eternal love scenario.
