The things that we’re really hardwired to seek: beauty, love, humour… I can’t prove to you that someone is beautiful. I can’t prove love even exists, and statistically we could make a case that it doesn’t exist. And we could say, like they talk about the God delusion, we could write a book called The Love Delusion. Why is everyone searching for this? No one has it. They’re all lying to themselves. It doesn’t really exist. But there’s an element inside us that can’t accept that it doesn’t exist.
