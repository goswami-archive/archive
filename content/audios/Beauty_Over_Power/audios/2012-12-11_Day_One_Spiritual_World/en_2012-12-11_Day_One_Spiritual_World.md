We have a stereotype that spiritual people should always be smiling. They always have a big smile on their face. Not so... It's just a typical day in Vṛndāvan, and Kṛṣṇa, Balarām, сowherd boys, cows, they've gone to the forest for the day. And the few hours, the hours they're gone, is an intolerable separation to the Vrij-vāsīs. That's why they're so miserable. They're feeling separation from Kṛṣṇa...
