Freud saying the sexual principle is driving everything; it’s driving the ants, it’s driving the human beings, you know, love makes the world go ’round. It’s the force that’s behind everything. And he has his system of interpretation for that. But see, what he’s expressing is a perverted reflection of the truth. The erotic principle, expressed in unalloyed devotion in the spiritual world, that’s the source of everything. The loving relationship between Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa is the source of everything, and this world is a perverted reflection of that...
