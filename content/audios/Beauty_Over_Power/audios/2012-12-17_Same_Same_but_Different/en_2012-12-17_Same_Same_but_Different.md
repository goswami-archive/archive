Captions: English, Russian, Chinese, Hungarian

Even the person in this world can simultaneously hold different identities, like a girl can be a daughter, a sister, a mother, a wife. She can hold those positions all simultaneously. From one group, or one perspective, the sister, the mother, the wife, the daughter – it is all possible. So that Krishna in all of his Divine Manifestations can hold multiple positions is not intangible? And following the principle of that – a part of the infinite is the infinite...
