This I find very interesting because we’re saying, “Mahāviṣṇu glanced”, like, “A twinkle in Mahāviṣṇu’s eye”, if you want to say. We understand this intuitively without getting too clinical about it. Where is the origin of all of us? Within the twinkle of an eye between a mother and a father! They looked at each other and then there was impregnation and birth... If you faithfully hear descriptions, you’ll see at a certain point, what sounded very mystical, abstract, inconceivable, starts mirroring common sense. That makes it all the more believable, accessible, and charming...
