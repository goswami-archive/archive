Captions: English, Russian, Spanish (Mexico), Hungarian

Everyone wants divinity to be within the realm of human comprehension, human feasibility; what we think is possible rationally. That’s why people are not interested in God, or want little 
or nothing to do with it. The God, the Kṛṣṇa we’re interested in is bewildering even to the demigods, can bewilder Brahmā, Indra, Śiva. Mahā-Viṣṇu wants a glimpse of Kṛṣṇa; wants to see the beauty of Kṛṣṇa, how beautiful he must be. Even other forms of Kṛṣṇa: want to see, have a glimpse... Krishna is All-Accommodating, All-Reciprocating. We shall resist trying to take the Infinite to fit in a little box in our puppy brain...
