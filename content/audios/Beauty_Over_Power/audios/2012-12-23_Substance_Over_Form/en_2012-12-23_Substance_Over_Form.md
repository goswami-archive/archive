Captions: English, Hungarian, Chinese (Simplified), Spanish (Mexico)

We’re interested in the substantial heartfelt appreciation of the devotion of others — not formalist formalities; that someone didn’t observe some formality, they violated a technicality. No one will be dismissed from Kṛṣṇa consciousness for violating a technicality. Rather, the rāga-bhaktas, they’re celebrated for ignoring those formalities.
