The magnitude of the sun is infinite and we can feel how hot it is
from 150 million kilometres away. So then what it would be like to go
to the centre of the sun? Long before we get there we'll disintegrate. So we can understand something in comparison,the magnitude of Kṛṣṇa's intensity of beauty, love, charm, and sweetness. Sunlike magnitude. So there's a particular position to be situated where we'll be nourished by that...
