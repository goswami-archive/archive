We’re not forbidden to know that madhura-rasa exists — the Divine pastimes of Kṛṣṇa and Rādhārāṇī, Vraja-gopīs… It’s not that we’re forbidden to know those things. What is forbidden is to attempt to go into the details; detailed analysis, detailed explanation. Because then, first of all it’s beyond our adhikār — our capacity, our eligibility, 
our qualification — and that ensures that aparādh, offence, will be committed along the way...
