Guru Mahārāj mentioned that Saraswatī Ṭhākur gave some mundane aspects of Ekādaśī, and some divine aspects. Part of fasting is to create a balance, because the enjoying tendency is stronger at this time by eating food. Either fasting, or eating foods that are non-stimulating, it’ll keep the balance of the aspiring servitor and let them stay focused on serving. But that just as our tendency for enjoyment is greater at this time, in the Reality, in the Supreme Reality, Kṛṣṇa’s tendency for enjoyment is greater at this time. So this is why the devotees are sacrificing a part of themselves, offering their energy to Kṛṣṇa for His enjoyment.
