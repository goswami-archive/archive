Captions: English, Russian, Hungarian, Spanish (Mexico)

You have to reconceive light. We know, to become enlightened,
illumined, that’s another type of luminosity; light, illumination,
is revelation. So what is light in the conscious plane? Without the conscious observer, it’s meaningless. So really it’s a position 
occupied by the soul, ātmā, and by extension, Paramātmā. Guru Mahārāj is saying, “And the halo of Śrīmati Rādhārāṇī is faith, and that which reveals Kṛṣṇa and Kṛṣṇa conception.” Her heart and halo; the halo is the outcome of Her heart, that is actually illuminating 
Kṛṣṇa conception and the service of Kṛṣṇa, and without that, it’s all imagination, fictitious, wallpaper, air bhajan.
