Guru Mahārāj brings the good news to us, “The Infinite can make Itself known to the finite, if He chooses.” Not by so much discussion, not by mere intellect, but He reveals Himself to whom He chooses through whom He chooses. And His favourite agency for distribution is His devotee agent.
