It’s very interesting, because we take it that guru is the functioning principle of divinity revealing Himself, but when He wants to teach devotion, He takes the position of Mahāprabhu; has to be under the influence of Śrīmati Rādhārāṇī to give a full, proper conception of Kṛṣṇa; a devotional conception of Kṛṣṇa. This is why Mahāprabhu descended into the world; to broadcast the glories of Rādhārāṇī.
