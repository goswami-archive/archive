Captions: English, Hungarian, Spanish (Mexico)

One day in the afternoon, there was Guru Mahārāj in his chair and Gurudev next to him and I came. Those were happy times. And I said to Gurudev, "Guru Mahārāj said that when Achyutānanda asked Swāmī Mahārāj, 'What did Śrīdhar Mahārāj say to you?' He said, 'If I told you, you would faint.' I said, "I want to know, what are those things?" And then Gurudev started laughing, And Guru Mahārāj said, "What, what?" and Gurudev told him in Bengali, "Goswāmī Mahārāj wants you to make him faint." And Guru Mahārāj [said], "Ācha." And they start talking, like, what should they say, fainting śloka...
