Captions: English, Russian, Hungarian, Chinese (Simplified), Spanish (Mexico)

The ultimate conception of the ultimate reality is beauty, and as Guru Mahārāj says, “Beauty is the ultimate controlling principle.” So, Mahāviṣṇu steals that brāhmaṇ’s boy.
Why? We find out in the Bhāgavatam. He wants to see the beauty of Kṛṣṇa.
