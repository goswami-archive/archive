There’s no formula, no method, no step and repeat procedure, that will generate spiritual substance. Matter cannot generate spirit, but spirit can manipulate matter. We say “Hare Kṛṣṇa” and then it purifies our hearts. The heart is under the influence of Sādhu, Śāstra, Guru, Vaiṣṇava..
