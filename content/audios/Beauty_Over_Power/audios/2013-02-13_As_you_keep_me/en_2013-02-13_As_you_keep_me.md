Captions: English

The vaiṣṇavas have this saying, when they enquire from one another, “How are you?”, they respond by saying, “As you keep me. The way you’re seeing me, viewing me, observing me, as I’m kept in your heart: that’s how I am, that’s what my position is.” They’re deferring to the other vaiṣṇava.
