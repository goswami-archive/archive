The first principle to understand is that Madan, Eros, Cupid, is creating moha, illusion, and that's why the illusory world is being driven by this principle, it's what makes it spin. But here, Madan's creating moha for the jīva-souls. Eros is creating illusion for everyone, it's the driving principal of material existence. Madan-mohan means, Kṛṣṇa is so beautiful, so seductively charming, that Kṛṣṇa is creating moha, illusion for Madan. Kṛṣṇa is putting Eros into illusion. Eros is being seduced by the Kṛṣṇa conception.
