Captions: English, Hungarian, Russian, Chinese (Simplified), Spanish (Mexico) 

Сreation is a secondary function performed by a part of a part of a part of His expansion with His eyes half-closed. Mahāviṣṇu generates innumerable Universes out of His pores with His eyes half-closed, half-asleep, He is lying in yoga-nidrā and His glance is personified as Śambhu Śiva, full of the sum total of jīvas and they are impregnated in the material nature.
