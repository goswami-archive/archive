Captions: English, Spanish (Spain)

This is what is most important, is that it is the will of Kṛṣṇa, to reveal himself to someone; it's Kṛṣṇa's will to make Himself known to someone. And that means to the degree, through a particular agency, all the different things that we may consider: in the background, it has to be Kṛṣṇa's will. And we're trying to sync our will with the will of Kṛṣṇa.
