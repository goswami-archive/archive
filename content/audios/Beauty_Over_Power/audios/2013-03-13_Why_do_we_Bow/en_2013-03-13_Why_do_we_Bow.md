Captions: English, Hungarian

We'll respectfully approach guru and inquire, ask a question,
on the basis of service necessity, unlike mundane knowledge
which is meant to serve you. Like someone goes to school, the classic example, they'll say, "Go to school, get educated, so you can get a good job. Your knowledge will serve you with financial reward." Here we're told, "You'll become a servant of the knowledge that is extended to you. It's not to serve your lower purpose, but now you're coming in connection with a sort of knowledge that will require you to serve its higher purpose."
