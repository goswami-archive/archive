Captions: English, Russian, Portuguese (Brazil), Hungarian, Spanish

Very often, most often, people think that you pray for something. They're praying for some divine intervention in their lives. A student is praying that they'll pass their exam; different things, different ideas are there. But we're told that's a lower type of prayer. Worshipping Kṛṣṇa, praying to Kṛṣṇa, is another thing all together. Still, people approach Kṛṣṇa to fulfil mundane aspirations.
