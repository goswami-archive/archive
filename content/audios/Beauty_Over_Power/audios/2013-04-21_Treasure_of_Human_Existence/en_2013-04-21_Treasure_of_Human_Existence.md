Captions: English, Russian. Chinese (Simplified)

We have a short time here to come in connection with the jewel of human life. The treasure of human existence is to get the association of genuine devotees—acyuta-gotra—those who are the family, family of Kṛṣṇa; we want their connection. If we are so fortunate, brahmāṇḍa bhramite kona bhāgyavān jīva’—[Cc: 2.19.151] wandering the length and breadth of the Universe to come in connection with them and their agents—then we’ve got the treasure that can be got from human existence.
