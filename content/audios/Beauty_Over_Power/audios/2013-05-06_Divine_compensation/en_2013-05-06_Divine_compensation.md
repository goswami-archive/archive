Captions: English, Russian, Spanish (Mexico), Chinese (Simplified), Italian, Hungarian

Kṛṣṇa says, “I personally compensate for any deficiencies that my devotees have.” So all this Harinam, the Nagar-saṇkīrtan, the many things the devotees did, the people on the other side, they do get something, and it’s real. And if there’s some fault on our part, Kṛṣṇa’s compensating for that. Even if they didn’t 100% get it right, the person was affected. In their heart they got something.
