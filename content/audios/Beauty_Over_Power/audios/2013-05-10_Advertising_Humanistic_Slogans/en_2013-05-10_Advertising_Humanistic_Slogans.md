Captions: English, Russian, Spanish (Mexico), Italian, Hungarian, Chinese (Simplified)

What’s real and what’s not real about self-expression? Even in a secular sense, artists, writers, musicians, different types of artists talk about finding their own voice under the layers and layers of cliché, of clichéd artistic expression. They talk about “trying to find your own voice”. Even they know, how you’re under the influence of those, who thought and expressed ideas before you. They know, for the most part, what people express is just their regurgitating what they’ve heard from others.
