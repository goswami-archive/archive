Captions: English, Hungarian, Italian.

"When organizing a big Gaura Purnima festival, Gurudev was arranging mosquito nets for the windows and when they finally arrived, Gurudev was smiling from ear to ear. He went upstairs with Guru Maharaj and said, "Now everyone will be happy, the mosquito nets have arrived." And Guru Maharaj looked up and said, "But the mosquito section, they will not be happy." He's even thinking of them. They have a place in his heart."
