Captions: English, Spanish, Hungarian

Guru Mahārāj once told me, “Analysis is a function of intellect.” Too much intellectualism creates doubt, doubt disturbs faith, and faith is our only way to understand this plane, and the personalities that populate that plane. This is what I want to say; that this should be at the basis of everything that we undertake. A faithful position. That, dedicating myself to the one, the many are understood.
