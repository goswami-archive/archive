Captions: English, Spanish

Kṛṣṇa, 'attracting everyone to the centre, with His irresistible charm, beauty and sweetness'; Kṛṣ-ṇa, 'and then nourishing everyone'. He's ecstasy personified. But who can extract the nectar? ... need nectar extraction. So many things are like that, they have some ... there's a method for how to extract. The method to extract the nectar of Kṛṣṇa is sevā, self-giving, self-offering.
