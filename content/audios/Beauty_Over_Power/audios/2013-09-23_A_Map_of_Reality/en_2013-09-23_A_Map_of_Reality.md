Captions: English

We think like that, so we hear, “Oh, Ayodhyā’s here, and Vaikuṇṭha’s over there, and Goloka ….” We can’t think of it in terms of time and space; we need to think of it in terms of conscious development and dedicating conscious tendency — that’s what separates spiritual regions from one another: a conscious development of dedicating tendency, not some topographical, cartographic type representation of it which an artist is likely to do if they make a painting — you know, put Goloka here, and then the Vaikuṇṭha world, and then …, like that.
