Captions: English

We find, in the beginning of the Bṛhad-bhāgavatāmṛtam, Mahārāj Janamejay is speaking with Jaimini Ṛṣi, and he'd just finished a Mahābhārata recitation, and Janamejay says, "I heard this previously from Vaiśampāyana Ṛṣi" — this story, the Mahābhārata divine story. He's saying, "Essentially you've told the same things, but the way you've told it, it has a particular madhura, sweetness, quality to it, and your recitation has awakened in me a desire for more of this madhura sweetness."
