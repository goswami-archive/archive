Captions: English

We have to understand everything in relationship and in connection with Kṛṣṇa... Everything can't be sweet. To really enhance the position of sweet, we begin the meal in Bengali style, Śuktā, something bitter: that will be tasteful, cleanse the palate, and amplify everything tasted after that...
