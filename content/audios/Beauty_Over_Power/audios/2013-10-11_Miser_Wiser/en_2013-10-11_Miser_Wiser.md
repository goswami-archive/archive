it's a rare opportunity, even biologically speaking — to take human birth. So you have this great wealth of opportunity, afforded by human life — athāto brahma jijñāsā — for spiritual inquiry. But you don't spend it on that, such a person is a kripana — a miser.
Whereas a brahman is someone, who's by the very nature inclined towards inner culture, spiritual culture, spiritual development. What others have a hard time getting their head around, brahmans understand intuitively.
