Mayavadis or impersonalists, they will say, "When you eat something very nice, you become silent". You get it? That's what they say. We don't say that. And that's not our experience. My experience is if you eat something very nice, you go like, "Wow!!! Did you have this? Did you get the sabji yet?" or, "Did you get the sweet? You've got to try it!"
