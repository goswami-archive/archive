Srila Trivikram Maharaj, he offered the five-miles one step, the five-miles the other step, and what remained for Govinda Maharaj? With his heart? So, when everything gave everything else — his family, property, everything — at last, he gave himself, his heart. And so, when he took sannyas, Gurudev named him Trivikram, which is the other name of Vamanadev. Tri-vikram: three steps, adbhuta-kram. He has wonderful stride as Vamana avatar. So, in the pranam, it will reference this sort of concepts. Hare Krishna
