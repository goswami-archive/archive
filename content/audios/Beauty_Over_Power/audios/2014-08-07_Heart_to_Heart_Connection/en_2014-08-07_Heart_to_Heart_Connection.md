Krishna only wants love and affection. 
So only the body is a Chinese body, a Thai body, American body, but there is not a Chinese soul, American soul, Thai soul, Indian soul... 
From the heart of Krishna to the heart of the devotee. And that transcends geography, politics, geopolitical, socio-economic circumstances.
