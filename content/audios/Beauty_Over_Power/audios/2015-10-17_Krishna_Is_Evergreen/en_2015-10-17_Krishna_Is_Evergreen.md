Kṛṣṇa is living, growing, developing, dynamic. Nitya-nava-navāyamāna. Nava-yauvana. Nava-yauvanaṁ ca [Bs: 5.33] Always newer, always fresher. Sometimes Guru Mahārāj would say, “Śyāmasundar means ‘eternally green; evergreen’.”
