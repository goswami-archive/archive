One of Śrīla Guru Mahārāj’s themes was ‘absolute consideration’ and ‘relative consideration’, and that always we have to factor in these two things. And he mentioned as well that Śrīla Saraswatī Ṭhākur particularly in his final days was fond of saying,“Religion Means Proper Adjustment.” So adjustment between absolute consideration and relative consideration.
