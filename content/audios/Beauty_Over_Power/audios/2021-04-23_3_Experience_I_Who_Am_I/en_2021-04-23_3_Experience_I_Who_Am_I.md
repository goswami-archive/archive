People think that they’re all about “self-expression”, and “I’ve just got to be me”. Sure, you should be you, but find out, who you are first. Don’t assume that what you are at present, under layers of acquired prejudice, acquired tendency and false ego, that false self, cocooning and enveloping the actual self, is the self that is worth all of this self-expression.
