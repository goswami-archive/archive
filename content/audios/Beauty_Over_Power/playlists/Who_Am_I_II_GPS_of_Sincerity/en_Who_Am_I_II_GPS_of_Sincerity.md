We take guidance for everything. Everybody watching us, or many people watching, they have GPS. They're not opposed to taking guidance, why? Because they understand the value of this device that will give indicators of how I should proceed. So for, you say a sincere person, a spiritually progressive person, they need spiritual GPS, which comes in the form of what we call Sadhu, Śastra, Guru, Vaiṣṇava; means spiritual teachers, the spiritual books, spiritual practitioners. You come in connection with them, put yourself under their guidance, and then, that revealed truth expressed by them becomes like a magnetic North, by which you can navigate the world and existence.
