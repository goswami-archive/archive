2015-07-13. Srila B. S. Goswami Maharaj.
Gupta Govardhan
Download audio here: http://mahamandala.com/en/audios/1112

Tolerating fake self
Can’t wish away our mind
Emphasis on service
Service to the Holy Name
Relief of worldly—mindedness
Your lotus feet are like the Sun
Praying to Guru and Vaishnavas for the help
Invoking auspiciousness
“Am I Chaitanya?”
Without Radharani Krishna is like Bramha
Self-oblivion without self-extinction
The ultimate self-discovery Physical proximity to the Guru is not so important
Learn to live in the plain of faith
Entering devotional current
Test Your mercy on me!
