2016-05-04. Dive Deep into Reality with Bhakti Sudhir Goswami
How pure is your aspiration

Q: When we receive initiation, Guru gives us a seed of devotion. Can we consider it to be energy, a drop of Srimati Radharani Herself? If so — can we think that Krishna is near our heart as He is always where Srimati Radharani is?

14:05 Q: I know that sometimes mercy comes from the higher plane, but we cannot receive it, because we are closed. Can it be that during the initiation our heart is so closed that it can’t receive bhakti-lata-bij?

22:17 Q: Does it mean that how much one is surrendered, that much he is initiated, regardless the formal procedure?

33:52 Q: One of the offenses to instruct a faithless person about the glories of the holy name. What does it mean in practice?

42:13 Q: Can we have spiritual desires at our practitioners’ stage? (If not) Does it mean that we should be aware that all force that drives us from within, all our inspiration is caused by mundane desires and then should be managed and directed by intelligence?

46:15 Q: A devotee always thinks that he has no spiritual capacity. So why should we even ask oneself (if my inspiration is real)? Why don’t just acknowledge that (all inspiration is mundane)?
