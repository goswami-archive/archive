2016-03-18. Dive Deep into Reality with Bhakti Sudhir Goswami
How to See the Higher Reality

Beginning of Navadvip-dham parikrama. Mahaprabhu gives the instructions to Rupa and Sanatana to go to Vrindavan and reveal holy places. Is this original Jagannath? — question irrelevant to the culture of faith. Don’t mix with the local people in Vrindavan, keep some distance and show respect for them.

Gurudev’s childhood cowherd songs and later understanding. Respect to all the animation in the Dham. When one’s spiritual eye is awakened, he can see the Dham.

Madhavendra Puri finds Gopala Deity. Revealed truth needs to be restored after some time as the original idea gets lost. Revolution is necessary. Narada chastising Vedavyas. Bhagavatam — pure evidence of the 5th goal of life (prema). Student and the teacher should become a system.

Baktivinod Thakur finds Mayapur. By his contribution, we can see he descended from the higher plane. Navadwip-dham-mahatmia.
Beginning of the spiritual process: hearing from the authorized agent of the Absolute: the devotees. Amnaya — not the product of human brain, imagination; spiritual sound descends in the this world.The Infinite can make itself known the finite. Everybody can appreciate Bhagavad-gita. Sanjaya can see the battlefield by the mercy of Vyasadev. Krishna consciousness it is not some artificial imposition on our mind.
