2016-05-13. Dive Deep into Reality with Bhakti Sudhir Goswami | Krishna, Ganga & Prasadam | "Limited Edition of the Infinite"
....

- How do the Vaishnavas celebrate Ganga Puja?
  Ganga is mentioned in Math pranam: it is located on Ganga banks. Everything is personal, because it comes from Radha and Krishna. Ganga Devi is inconceivably beautiful goddess represented in this plane in the form of the river. How Ganga came to Earth. Gangadhar Sada Shiva is carrying Ganga on his head.

Purifying capacity is extraordinary: even if you see something polluting, it doesn’t contaminate Ganga. Pundarik Vidyanidhi worshiped Ganga with her water.

Human civilization is based on river culture. We can’t properly estimate, how much love the genuine Vaishnavas have for mother Ganges. She is infinite.

- How Ganga gave back Srila Gurudev’s kavaca (amulet).
  Why do we use Ganga in so many temple activities?
  She is dear to Krishna. Tulasi leaf and Ganga water occupy high position.

Krishna is yagya-bhuk: He eats what was offered during the sacrifice ceremony.

By eating food that has been offered to Krishna, you will be free from karmic reactions. By honoring prasadam you will conquer over the mundane world.

Brahma-vimohan lila.
