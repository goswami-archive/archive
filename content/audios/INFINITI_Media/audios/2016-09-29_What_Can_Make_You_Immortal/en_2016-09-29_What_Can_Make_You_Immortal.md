2016-08-19. Dive Deep into Reality with Bhakti Sudhir Goswami
What can make you immortal?
|| Q: If one engages in social works like helping the sick, feeding the poor, etc. will this result in having sukriti? Does this path lead also to Krishna consciousness? Is there a difference between pious work and Krishna-seva?

|| Q: What happens to those who come in connection with the Vaishnavas in offensive mood? Do they also get some kind of sukriti?

|| Q: In Sri Isopanisad we read: Om purnam adah purnam idam—whatever is produced of the Complete Whole is also complete in itself. And Srila Sridhar Maharaj also says that a part of the Infinite is infinite. How come that we, being the parts of the Infinite are limited, at the same time? Is it achintya?
