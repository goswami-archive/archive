2016-05-18. Dive Deep into Reality with Bhakti Sudhir Goswami
A Place in the Heart of a Vaishnava

|| Q: Once, I heard an expression: to find a place in a Vaishnava’s heart. Does it depend on the Vaishnava’s will, mercy or on the qualification of the devote? Is this position constant?

Krishna says, The devotees are in My heart. Those devotees do not allow even a pinprick through which any remuneration can pass to them. Don’t try to approach Krishna directly. Take shelter of His devotees and serve them. And Krishna will be pleased with you.
In the heart of Saraswati Thakur – Bhaktivinoda Thakur. So Guru Maharaj praised Bhaktivinoda Thakur— that’s the way to the heart Saraswati Thakur.
Vaishnava is the one who has recognition of the other Vaishnavas.
