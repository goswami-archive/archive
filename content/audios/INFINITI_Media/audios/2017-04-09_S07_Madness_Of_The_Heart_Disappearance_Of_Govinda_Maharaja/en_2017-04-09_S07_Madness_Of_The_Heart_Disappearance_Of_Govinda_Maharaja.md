S07 • Madness of the Heart. Disappearance of Govinda Maharaja

— Without the devotees Krishna conception is suspect — it could be imaginary
— See Guru not as imperfect man, but as a divine delegation
— Vaishnavas appear in this world for some time and then withdraw themselves — to awaken our serving potential
— After the disappearance, the real test comes: am I a real disciple?
— Pratishtha is offered to the lotus feet of the Guru
