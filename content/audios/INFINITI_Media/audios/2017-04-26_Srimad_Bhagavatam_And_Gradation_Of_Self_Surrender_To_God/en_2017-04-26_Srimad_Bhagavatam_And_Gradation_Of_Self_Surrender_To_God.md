27 Mar 2017.
— Q: Parikshit Maharaja is told that 7 days is enough to achieve life's perfection. But we are also told that it's a life after life practice. How to understand that 7 days is enough?
— Quality trumps quantity.
— Imperfect senses: we are prone to commit mistakes.
— Super-sensuous: beyond the range of your present senses, yet sensual — adhokshaja.
— Pass the Socrates test: I'm spirit and the spirit doesn't die.
— Madhura-rasa — the ultimate nectar that gives service.
