28 Oct 2016.

- How did the recent Vrindavana Parikrama go?
- The Kartika month, the month of Srimati Radharani.
- The careful approach to the Holy places.
- Uddhava: being a blade of grass in Vrindavan.
- Gadadhar Pandit: Mahaprabhu didn't allow the Vrindavana Queen to enter Vrindavan.
- Jagadananda Pandit: even he was given caution about how to behave in Vrindavan.
- Where should one start Vrindavana Parikrama?
- Srila Rupa Goswami gave Srila Govinda Maharaj land in the heart of Vrindavan.
- Ten meetings with Guru.
- Srila Prabhupada in Radha-Damodara temple.
- Srila Prabhupada sailing 30 days on "Jaladuta" to fulfill Srila Saraswati Thakur's instructions.
- "Preaching of Bhagavata Dharma in America" and "Prayer to the lotus feet of Krishna."
- "Make me dance, O Lord!"
- Srila Prabhupada engaged Krishna in the service to his Guru (Srimati Radharani).
- "If you dedicate your life to Krishna, don't expect to make a parade in your honour."
- Krishna made a parade for Srila Prabhupada.
