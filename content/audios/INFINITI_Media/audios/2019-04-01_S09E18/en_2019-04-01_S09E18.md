Q: “The paradox in the nutshell is this..." writes the journalist Oliver Morton in the “Planet Remade” 2015. "Humans are grown so powerful that they have become a force of Nature and forces of Nature are those things which by definition are beyond the power of humans to control.”
How far or what destination may acquired prejudices and acquired tendencies of mankind take us? Is the catastrophe inevitable or we still have a chance to avoid it?"
