Q: If the bhakti-lata-bija is not properly nurtured and protected, and it withers, what should one do? Can it be revived by getting initiation again?

— A moment of direct connection to Krishna is infinitely better than all other methods combined
— Describe the pure lovers of Krishna!
— Seeing through the eyes of the devotees
— Awakening, development of bhakti-lata-bija
— Enemies of devotion
— By the grace of the devotees, the seed can be revived
— Reinitiation should not become a fashion
— Everything comes by the grace of the devotees
— The curiosity to experience devotional position was awakened in Krishna by association with the supreme devotee, Srimati Radharani
— How shall Krishna transition to a devotee?
— Brahma and Siva in the guise of humans come to see Mahaprabhu
— Naughty Nimai becomes a devotee
— Devotional tendencies awaken in Mahaprabhu after return from Gaya
— Advaita Acharya’s prayers to the Lord
— Mahaprabhu appears to His devotees through kirtan
— Opposition is necessary to enhance the position of the devotee

— A taste for the pure Name of Krishna
— Krishna appears before Mahaprabhu in Kanai-Natshala and then disappears
— The real devotion is about the search

— Mahaprabhu knows the hearts of all His devotees
