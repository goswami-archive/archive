S10E31 • Madan-Mohan Krishna

Q: Very often we hear about ten million Cupids in their full-blown cupidity who faint seeing the beauty of Lord Gaurasundar. Also in praṇām-mantram to the Deities of Sanātan Goswāmī, we meet the name of Madana-Mohan applied to the Lord. While the concept of Eros as the driving principle is more or less conceivable, the personality of Cupid remains beyond the grasp. Who is that Cupid, and what position is He taking?

— Polar opposites: Cupid and Madan-Mohan Krishna

— The torch of rational thought

— Plurality of theistic systems
— Rasa is the absolute standard of theism
