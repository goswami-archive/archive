Q: I was recalling a line from a śloka dedicated to Śrīla Sanātan Goswāmī saying, ‘he forced me to drink the nectar of rasa.’ Finally, devotees helped me to find the whole śloka, a Praṇām Mantra composed by Śrīla Raghunāth Dās Goswāmī.
vairāgya-yug-bhakti-rasaṁ prayatnair
apāyayan mām anabhīpsum andham
kṛpāmbudhir yaḥ para-duḥkha-duḥkhī
sanātanas taṁ prabhum āśrayāmi
[Vilāpa-kusumāñjali: 6]
Could You please explain this idea: ‘forced to drink the nectar?’
