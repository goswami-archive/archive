Srimati Bhakti Lalita's class
for Sri Nityananda Prabhu Appearance day at Gupta Govardhan, Chiang Mai, Thailand.

Audio (listen/download): https://mahamandala.com/en/audios/1511

Srimati Bhakti Lalita Devi Dasi is disciple of Srila Bhakti Sundar Govinda Dev-Goswami Maharaj of 20+ years. Born in the USA, she lived in India with her spiritual master and served as his personal secretary. In 1996 she was given the sacred saffron brahmachari cloth, a revolutionary adjustment made for women for the first time by Srila Govinda Maharaj, and the title "Bhakti" was added before her initiated name, "Lalita Devi Dasi". She is now based in Chiang Mai, Thailand and travels the world encouraging the hearts and nurturing the faith of devotees and friends worldwide. She gives regular morning classes on the philosophy of Gaudiya-vaishnava culture and the practice of Bhakti-yoga.
