Q: As we know, Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu descends once in a day of Brahmā. It is Krishna Himself who decides to experience that type of love that She alone possesses, especially when She feels heartbroken and runs away from Him into the forest. Does it turn out that She runs away from Him every day of Brahmā? And is Krishna every time anew interested in Her love for Him?
