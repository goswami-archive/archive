DAY 8. Vasudev Ghosh, Uddhava Kunda, Kusum Sarovar • B.S. Goswami & B.R. Madhusudan

Vasudev Ghosh; if Gauranga had not come—how would we live; deceptive simplicity of Vrindavan; separation and union: how long is the blink of an eye; family life with Krishna; Gopi-gita; cure for all suffering is hari-katha; Bhaktivinode Thakur and Saraswati Thakur's bhajan-kutir; Mahaprabhu's resting place; Kusum Sarovar: serving to the higher plane and then come back.
