S11E59 • A Verse that Will Make Everyone Happy

— Ramananda Das; establishing Mahaprabhu’s lotus feet on the banks of Godavari; Ramananda Ray comes to Puri; Mahaprabhu’s fire bed of separation; before Sri Chaitanya astakam — quoting Prema-dhama-deva-stotram; the poetical decorations

00:06:22 Sri Sri Prema-dhama-deva-stotram, verse one: who worships Mahaprabhu;

00:12:04 Shyamasundar Krishna became akrishna, golden;
— Krishna converted into a devotee;

00:14:08 The fire of mundane suffering is extinguished;
— Pāpa-tāpa — saṁsāra-dāvānala; romāñcha-kampāśru; dāna-sāgar — His gift is the abode of divine nectar, the Holy Name of Krishna; we pray only to Gaurasundar (and that’s only verse one;

00:18:54 Rupa Goswami in Puri
— Humility has no aggression; Siddha Bakul: Haridas Thakur, Sanatan Goswami and Rupa Goswami lived there; Mahaprabhu’s remnants were brought to them; chana-avatar — enveloped by the heart of Radharani — hidden in a delightful way — but not hidden to the devotees; Ramananda-samvad: Mahaprabhu appeared on the banks of Godavari like ten million suns; triumph of substance over form; kibā vipra, kibā nyāsī — if someone knows Krishna’s science — he’s a guru; Advaita Acharya called Gauranga not for himself — for the suffering people — that’s what was reciprocated;

00:28:03 Krishna comes as Gopis’ canvasser;
— Mahabhava; distributing Himself in the form of His name; “the transcendental sound vibration form of the Lord”; Rupa Goswami’s shloka for Istha-devata; other avatars can give the yuga-dharma, but once in a day of Brahma comes Krishna who can give prem; unnatojjvala-rasām — something that was never given before; the most magnanimous avatar — gives the highest to the lowest; public service and private life of Mahaprabhu;

00:38:57 A happy shloka by Rupa Goswami that will finish all the miseries of our lives (he can write such a verse);
Prabhupad said about tri-bhaṅga posture of Krishna — “as if He has nothing to do”, He has no job, predisposed to play; He controls everything with His flute;

00:43:13 Gopis think, “The cause of our trouble is the flute”;
— Rasa-rāja mahā bhāva — dui eka rūpa — Ramananda sees Shyama-gopa-rupa, enveloped by the golden heart; the non-devotees cannot see Him; He’s totally independent — you can tell by tri-bhaṅga; in the presence of the devotees it’s as if Krishna loses His independence (they have not half-heartedly given themselves); humanists shudder at “slave”, being the slaves of their own senses; kṛṣṇa-nāma-sīdhu-dhāma — Krishna-nam is the abode of all nectar — one tongue is not enough; you can only learn it from a devotee;

00:51:09 Rupa Goswami tells the effects of Krishna’s flute;

00:56:00 Guru Maharaj explains three gifts of Mahaprabhu;
— The positive gift — that divine name; the tongue attacked by the disease of ignorance: the divine name is not tasteful to me; durdaivam — my misfortune is I have no taste for the holy name — but the devotees say that millions of tongues are necessary to taste the nectar; to think you are a Vaishnava is to be on the road to hell; we don’t think we are that — but we know that such devotees exist and we’re aspiring servants of their servants; deploring their own construction (only one mouth); misconception: the tasting sense is covered with clay — so no direct contact with honey; the sweetness of the Name; barren heart; bhakti-lata will go to Krishna and roll on His feet; then comes the Chaitanya astakam. That’s the intro.
