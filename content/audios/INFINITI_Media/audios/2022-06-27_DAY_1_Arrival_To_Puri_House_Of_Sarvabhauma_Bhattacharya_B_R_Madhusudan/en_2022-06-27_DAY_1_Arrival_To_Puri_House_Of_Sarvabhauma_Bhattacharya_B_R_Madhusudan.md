June 26, 2022
Everything in Jagannatha Puri is centered around Hari-nam; Bengal devotees came to Mahaprabhu before Jagannath;

The real substance is in the search; juicy and delightful; the Eastern savior; nadīyā-udayagiri; Jagannath prasad: liberating mercy for everyone; appearance of Saraswati Thakur; nam-sankirtan is madhurya-rasa in distribution mode; Nadadwip and Puri relations;
Gauranga's return to Nabadwip; Maharaj means "a great king": a parallel of Mahaprabhu and Krishna's pastimes; Gopis are not satisfied with Dwaraka Krishna: "What's the fun?!"; Vrindavan is the construct of Radharani's heart; the inseparable paraphernalia of the divine pastimes; Nimai Pandit's gradual "evolution" from bhakta-bhav to radha-bhav; meaning of the name Krishna Chaitanya; take sannyas and live with your Mom?; solace for mother Sachi; Advaita Acharya releases Mahaprabhu from public service;

Rupa Goswami's verse that reflects Mahaprabhu's emotions; radiance of a blue sapphire; value of the search; eight types of ecstacy experienced simultaneously; on the way to Puri: Remuna, Krishna-premankur in the heart of Madhavendra Puri, the suprem shloka of rasa-kavya (din and doya); when devotees reach a certain stage of separation - then Krishna reveals Himself;

Mahaprabhu arrives to Jagannatha Puri; chakra of the temple: "Gopal's looking at Me"; a 9-hour trance in front of Jagannath; hidden avatar in the house of Sarvabhauma Bhattacharya; when self-giving current is in the heart - then Krishna may reveal Himself; a 7-day Vedanta discourse for Mahaprabhu by Sarvabhauma Bhattacharya; explanations of atmarama shloka; convertion of the court pandit into raga-bhakta; Maharaj Prataparudra observes devotees from the roof; tha savior of Gajapati and Maharaj Prataparudra.
