Devotees are always humble and appreciate the devotion of others; the best brands market their own competition; praising Krishna's flute through the fault-finding; from Krishna-lila to the Bollywood movies; Krishna's exclusive territory: madhurya-rasa; intrinsic prospect of love in the spiritual world;

disappointment on Kurukshetra; Dwaraka Krishna has to apologize; family life with Krishna; Yashoda's fortune; Dwaraka queens har about Vrindavan from Rohini; Jagannath is Dwaraka Krishna in the intense separation from Vrindavan;

all life stages in one train car; a white man emtering Jagannath Temple; Dayita Madhav Maharaj meets Goswami Maharaj and tells about Puri parikrama; who is Siddha-bakul; Siddha-bakul - chakra - Sweta Ganga - Purushottam Gaudiya Math (Chatak Parvat); the punchline is always Krishna; gambhira - diving deep into reality; vahni-garba - the firebed of separation (Prema-dhama-deva-stotram); irresistable ecstasy;

Chatak Parvat is Gupta Govardhan; praise of Govardhan: the best of the servants of Hari; little ponds of Krishna-prem; Krishna-nam of the pure devotees is given preference over the direct participation in the lila.
