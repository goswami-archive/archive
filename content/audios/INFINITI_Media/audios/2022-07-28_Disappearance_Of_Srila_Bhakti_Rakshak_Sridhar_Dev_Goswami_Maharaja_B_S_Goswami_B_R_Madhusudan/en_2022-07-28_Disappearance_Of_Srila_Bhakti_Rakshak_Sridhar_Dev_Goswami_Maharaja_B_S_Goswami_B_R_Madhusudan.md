July 28, 2022
Goswami Maharaja
Worship Ganga with Her waters: enter Sridhar Maharaja’s current to worship him; first connection due to the disappearance of Srila Swami Maharaja Prabhupad; Krishna is inconceivably intense: Guru is like water, disciple is like lotus; maintain your position here with your aspiration there: Math pranam;

Das Goswami’s sentiments take separation described in Siksastakam to a new level; we are Guru-centric; Swami Maharaja’s words on Sri Guru: “There is only one guru, who appears in an infinity of forms”; Saraswata current, substance and form; Ramananda-samvad: the ontological method of Sridhar Maharaja;

“Sridhar Maharaja knows everything”: first years in the Gaudiya Math; “Sri_Rupa-manjari-pada”: the mystic transition of rupanuga line from Saraswati Thakur to Sridhar Maharaja; prayers to Bhaktivinod Thakur that capture the heart of Sarasawati Thakur; “Sri Chaitanya-charitamrita”: the greatest literature, based on the notes of Swarup Damodar and Raghunath Das Goswami; the current of divine substance can be modified or protected; the service of the holy feet of Srimati Radharani (Krishna consciousness) is Bhaktivinod’s gift to the world; Swami Prabhupada directed his disciples to Sridhar Maharaja, whom he considere to be his siksha-guru;

Madhusudan Maharaja
Earthquake in Nabadwip: Bhumi Devi shuddering at the prospect of Sridhar Maharaja’s disappearance; torrential rain on the eve of Guru Maharaja’s disappearance; one link in the chain of translation: Sridhar Maharaja gave us everything in good English; Swami Prabhupad and Guru Maharaja’s close relation; chastity to Guru: we will get everything from Sridhar Maharaja—the pillar of Krishna consciousness worldwide;

Goswami Maharaja
After Saraswati Thakur’s disappearance Sridhar Maharaja compiles “Prapanna-jivanamritam”; contemplating where to spend his days: Uddhav-khyari, Puri or Nabadwip; entering Nabadwip through Nitai-bari in Ekachakra; place where all the offenders are forgiven; 80th anniversary of establishing SCSM; Math pranam; Radharani’s heart captured Krishna and converted Him into Gauranga Mahaprabhu; parallels with Das Goswami: maintain your position in this world and your aspiration to the higher plane; “Prema-Dhama-Deva-stotram”; Guru Maharaja revealed Radha-dasyam from Brahma Gayatri; the subjective evolution of consciousness—fresh conceptions, mining the current and extending it to us; the original concepts of Sridhar Maharaja; Search for Sri Krishna, Reality the Beautiful; Sri Guru and His Grace; Golden Volcano of Divine Love; Loving Search for the Lost servant: the Lord is looking for His servants; Subjective Evolution; the Hidden Treasure of the Sweet Absolute; the pull of affection in the disappearance can take us to that plane.
