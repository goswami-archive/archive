Swarup Damodar is ratna-sar bandar: everything in Gaura-lila comes from him; in Krishna-lila Svarup Damodar is Lalita Sakhi; an advocate of Srimati Radharani; the noble opposition in the pastimes; svarupa-nugas; first verse of Lalita-ashtakam of Rupa Goswami explained; Lalita Devi’s total self-sacrifice for the least aspect of seva; the plane where someone can chastise Krishna; canvassing in Vrindavan; Shyamananda Prabhu’s story;

Unparalleled assistance to Srimati Radharani; Anuradha; Swarup Damodar is Mahaprabhu’s confident; Svarup’s Raghu: Raghunath Das Goswami’s under the care of Svarup Damodar; suddha brij-basi: the real opulence: Vrindavan and Vaikuntha; Srila Govinda Maharaja’s laser-like focus; Ucha Gaon – the place of Lalita Sakhi, Lalita-bari;
