Sep 4, 2022
First verse of “Radha-rasa-sudha-nidhi” by Prabhodananda Saraswati.
Kirtan by Sridhar Maharaja and Govinda Maharaja: “Radhe jaya jaya Madhava-dayite”, “Virajar pare suddha-paravyoma-dham” “Radhika-charana-padma”, “Radhakundatata-kunjakutira”, “Radha-bhajane jodi moti nahi bhela”.

The nicknames of the Divine couple: Kan and Rai; Krishna consciousness is the celebration of the heart of Srimati Radharani; Krishna-bhajan should culminate in Radha-bhajan; Kaviraj Goswami writes Sri Chaitanya-charitamrita; the lighthouse of Krishna-prem; faith is the halo of Srimati Radharani; Madhya 2,3 – Chaitanya-charitamrita in the condensed form; conspiracy to capture Krishna’s flute;

“Radha-rasa-sudha-nidhi” – the title is already too much; Svayam Bhagavan Krishna’s at the right hand of Srimati Radharani; Saraswati Thakur: “The best day of the year”; a shift from Krishna to the one who is Prabhu Priya; Gauranga is Krishna who celebrates the heart of Srimati Radharani; sankirtan of Mahaprabhu is rasa-lila in distribution mode; search for Sri Krishna in Vrindavan; message of the Bhagavatam is to take us to the service of Her lotus feet; separation yield a higher type of Krishna-prem; Gopi meets Krishna at Kurukshetra; faint-inducing beauty of Radharani; remuneration beyond the soul’s capacity; a plate of maha-bhav; Guru Maharaj: “Just Gauranga and Radharani”; the highest nectar is service to the lotus feet of Srimati Radharani
