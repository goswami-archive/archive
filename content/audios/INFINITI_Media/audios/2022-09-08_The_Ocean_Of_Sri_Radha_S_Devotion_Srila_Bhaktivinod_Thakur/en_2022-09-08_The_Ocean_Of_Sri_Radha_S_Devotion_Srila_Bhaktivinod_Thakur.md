Sep 8, 2022
Appearance Day of Srila Sachchidananda Bhaktivinode Thakur
00:00:00 Vandanam

00:01:37 Atma-nivedana is the most important aspect of sharanagati; self-giving should be at the basis of all [these things] to be qualified as devotion; Krishna is playful; Vamanadev asks Bali Maharaj for three steps of land; “Give Krishna what He wants!”; Vamana Dev doesn’t want a Universe, but Bali Maharaja’s heart;
00:19:31 Srila Jiva Goswami: the protector of Rupa-nuga line; defeating Digvijaya Pandit and Vallabha Bhatta;

00:29:08 Bhaktivinode-paribar of Saraswat Math; Bhaktivinod Thakur is the head; what’s coming from Mahaprabhu — that’s what comes from Bhaktivinod Thakur; faith is our only access to that plane; looking for Krishna in the Vedas; what our Gurus are giving is superior to Vedas; the worship of Gaur-Gadadhar is the private bhajan chamber of Bhaktivinod Thakur; bringing Saraswati Thakur down to this plane; Bhaktivedanta Swami Maharaj and Sridhar Maharaj — the disciples of Saraswati Thakur; Yoga Pith discovery; the place of Raghunath Das Goswami in Radha Kunda is the North-East corner; the schedule of Bhaktivinod Thakur; api cet su-duracaro verse explanation: if a pure devotee does something abominable (no one applies it to themselves as an excuse); the blessing of Svarup Damodar

00:52:47 Sridhar Maharaja’s strategy to capture Saraswati Thakur is to glorify Bhaktivinod Thakur; “Bhaktivinoda-viraha Dasakam”: the gift of Bhaktivinod Thakur to the world; Kamala Manjari, Nayana Mani Manjari, Guna Manjari in the spiritual world; jivadyair abhiraksitam; the ocean of the service to the lotus feet of Srimati Radharani: Radha-rasa-sudha-nidhi; last days of Bhaktivinod Thakur in this world: the nectar of the holy Name; sri-radha-pada-sevanamrtam aho tad-datum iso bhavan; the full wealth of Krishna consciousness was given to the world by Srila Bhaktivinod Thakur;

01:06:36 “Sri Nama”(gay gora madhur svare) by Bhaktivinod Thakur
