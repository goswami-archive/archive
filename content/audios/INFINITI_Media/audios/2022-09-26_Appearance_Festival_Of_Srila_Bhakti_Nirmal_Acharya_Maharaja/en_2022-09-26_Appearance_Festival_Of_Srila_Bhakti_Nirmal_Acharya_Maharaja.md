Sep 26, 2022
00:00:00 Not to be envious and glorify the devotees; the primary position of a devotee; Kaviraj Goswami is made of the mercy of Nityananda Prabhu; Vrindavan is the world where the devotee is in the superior position; Krishna is subordinate to the devotion;
Pranam of Govinda Maharaja; Sridhar Maharaja’s dream of Gaur and Nitai playing in the waters of Govinda Kunda; Srila Acharya Maharaj is “the impossible man”; jal-krida, the divine water pastimes; the draining of Govinda Kunda and the churning of the Milk Ocean; Acharya Maharaj built the temple and took Mahaprabhu on the swan raft;

00:27:31 Nirascarya; “stealing” money from Govinda Maharaj for the Bamunpara temple; “something enters his seva circle and just becomes realized”; Ekachakra temple; Tarakeshwar – city where Bhaktivinod Thakur had the vision of Mayapur; ascharya – astonishing; excellent cook;

00:44:21 Bhakti Lalita Devi Dasi
Doing the needful service; making Govinda Maharaj worry-free and allowing him to go on the world tours; never be lazy.
