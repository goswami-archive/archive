Mar 3, 2023 | Day 1. Sri Nabadwip Dham Online Parikrama • Srila B.S. Goswami & Srila B.R. Madhusudan
— Summary:
Srila Goswami Maharaj | Vandana with tempura.
First parikramas: Nityananda Prabhu and Jiva Goswami, Narottam Thakur and Srinivas Acharya; the things that cannot hold center fall apart – disintegration of Krishna consciousness; the yoga that’s not linking the individual consciousness with the supreme consciousness is not yoga proper; the truth is inevitably converted into error; if “it’s baked in the cake” then a new revelation is necessary; a useful student is like a satellite – reflecting light from the source; Bhaktivinod Thakur is the system, not a satellite and we are the aspiring servitors of Bhaktivinod-paribar (family);

Saraswati Thakur revived Nabadwip Dham parikrama; Saraswati Thakur didn’t sing or dance himself, but infused others with such sentiments; popular sahajias – who is the best counterfeiter; ever-merciful Bhaktivinod Thakur appears among the sahajias, the transcendental batul, madman;
The line is from Mahaprabhu to Saraswati Thakur; Guru Maharaj is absolutely right; when the link is broken, your iPhone 13 is useless; the link is broken by substitution (sarvopādhi-vinirmuktaṁ); keep mission united – for what?; how can one who never read Sridhar Maharaja’s books can be the Sevaite-Acharya of SCSM?;

When someone told Sridhar Maharaj what “Bhaktisiddhanta wants”; siddhanta is Reality (talks about the realty scare people away); three pure devotees obsessed with the shlokas; if something is meant as prohibition, it means, it’s going on extensionally; starting off worshipping Mother Yashoda and end up thinking you’ve become her; my Krishna – madhu-sneha; Stanislavskiy method; not divine tears; useful to know about the sahajiyas to be protected; Saraswati Thakur was blowing his disciples’ minds regularly;

Bhaktivinod Thakur had 5-7 disciples and his gifts are everything that we have; special prayers to Bimala Devi resulted in Bimala Prasad; vision of Mayapur during the stop in Tarakeshwar; My-a-pur; oh my Gauranga!; a confirmation of finding Mayapur by Sarvabhauma Vaishnav – Jagannath Das Babaji Maharaj; kirtan – Babaji Maharaja’s mercy – he shared his ecstasy;

Saraswati Thakur is drawn by the wealth in the melted heart of Bhaktivinod Thakur; not a blood line – “look for me in the material world”; more European than an English gentleman; transfer of seva-puja in Mayapur; “jungly” Mayapur; starting to do seva-puja at 45; kirtan, hari-katha, prasadam; nam-sankirtan, sadhu-sanga (Vaishnava-seva), Bhagavat-sravan (hear Bhagavatam and from a person Bhagavatam), why to stay in a place called Dumdum Park?; sleeping between two generators, etc. – don’t tell me about your discomforts; where Mahabhagavata is – there Mathura-bas is; “devotees of your statue create holy places”; our temples are Vaikuntha embassies; tuṣyanti ca ramanti ca; archan and bhajan: Sanantan Goswami is not worshipping, he’s talking to [a sweetly annoying] Madan Mohan; when Govinda Maharaja put Mahaprabhu’s Deity to Sridhar Maharaja’s Samadhi, he considered a lot of things first;

Srila Madhusudan Maharaj.
Antardwip, Mayapur, Ekadashi day, ready to serve the devotees; nine islands, nine petals of the Nabadwip lotus; Gauranga-consciousness; the era of Mahaprabhu;

Kirtan led by Srila Bhakti Sudhir Goswami Maharaja.
