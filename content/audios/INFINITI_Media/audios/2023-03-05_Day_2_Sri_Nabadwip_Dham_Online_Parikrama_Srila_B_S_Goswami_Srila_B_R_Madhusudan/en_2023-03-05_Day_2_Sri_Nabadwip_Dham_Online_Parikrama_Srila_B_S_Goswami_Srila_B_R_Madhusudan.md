Mar 4, 2023 | Day 2. Sri Nabadwip Dham Online Parikrama • Srila B.S. Goswami & Srila B.R. Madhusudan
— Summary:
Srila Madhusudan Maharaja.

From Yogapith to Koladwip; Sri Chaitanya Saraswat Math; a half-day parikrama.

Srila Bhakti Sudhir Goswami Maharaja.

Sridhar Maharaj appreciated self-sacrifice the most; the beautiful destroying dance of Siva Nataraj; “Dance on me!” – devotees offer themselves to the Lord; “to one whom Krishna favours, He takes away everything”; Sridhar Maharaj joins Gaudiya Math; “Love and rapture – both for Krishna”; stay in your position to make progress until it hinders your progress and you go down;

Expressing gratitude to Sridhar Maharaja by giving that to others and not anything less than that; you don’t exhaust everything that can be said about the shloka – you’re dealing with the Infinite; according to the different capacity of the receivers; “Sri Sri Prapanna-jivanamritam” is compiled of verses about sharanagati; a drop of Krishna-katha will ruin you forever – you’ll have to pretend to be a member of the material world; your material life is finished, today or tomorrow, so do some good things on the way out;

Sridhar Maharaja’s specialty is something (brahmar-gita) that only a handful of people can understand; Sridhar Maharaja’s choice of the retirement place: Kadam-kandi, Uddhav-khyari of Koladwip; receiving the permission of Nityananda Prabhu in Ekachakra; sahajiyas have photoshoped visas for the spiritual world; an inspiration from Nityananda Prabhu: give shelter to the fallen; pratishtha is like pig shit;

Koladwip is the original Nabadwip; Srila Govinda Maharaj commissioned the Deities for Nabadwip; Govinda Maharaj didn't put anything into his mouth without saying, "Jay Guru Maharaj!"; only Govinda Maharaj called Srila Prabhupada "Swami Maharaj"; Bhagavan Bhaktisiddhanta Saraswati Thakur; putting the deity of Mahapraprabhu into Sridhar Maharaja's samadhi;

seva of the acharya: to extract new meaning from the scriptures; Govinda Maharaj was reluctant to become Sridhar Maharaja's successor and Acharya; 9th dome of SCSM is ātma-nivedanam; Govinda Maharaja's idea on construction; "I'm your goat"; gurvvābhīṣṭa-supūrakaṁ; ever the servitor of Srila Sridhar Maharaj;

Vishnupriya Devi's Mahaprabhu Deity, offering gesture, fatty from all the prasadam; deities in Italy: mischievous Krishna; as a rule: dancing posture of Mahaprabhu;
