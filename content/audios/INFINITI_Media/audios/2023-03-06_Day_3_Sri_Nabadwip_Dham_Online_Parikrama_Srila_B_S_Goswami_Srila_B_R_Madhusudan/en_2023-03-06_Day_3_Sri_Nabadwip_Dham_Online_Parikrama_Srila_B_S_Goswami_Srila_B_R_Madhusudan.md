Mar 5, 2023 | Day 3. Sri Nabadwip Dham Online Parikrama • Srila B.S. Goswami & Srila B.R. Madhusudan
— Summary:
Under the banyan tree near Srila Bhaktivinod Thakur’s samadhi;

Srila Bhakti Sudhir Goswami

Bhaktivinod Thakur’s timetable; a pure devotee never does anything wrong – if you can believe that – quickly you’ll become dharmic; you cannot self-certify; Guru Maharaj prefers to tell the truth and accept the consequences of that; you shouldn’t try to purify your Guru;

The gifts of Bhaktivinod Thakur show that he descended from the highest plane; Bhaktivinod Thakur needed a distributing agent and that is Saraswati Thakur; Lalita Prasad; Saraswati Thakur’s style of proving his statement; entertainment for the pilgrims; “Birds of the Dham”;

Glories of Kunja Babu; the nectarine service of the lotus feet of Srimati Radharani is Bhaktivinod Thakur’s gift to the world; “Bhaktivinod-viraha-dashakam”; rupa-nuga-dhara; sva-pada-mula shloka; lying and cheating for the Guru is worshipable;

Bhaktivinod Thakur’s prediction that Srila Prabhupada made come true; Govinda Maharaja’s speech at the opening of the Chandrodaya Mandir, “Whom can I tell and who would believe it?”; “We are happy, we are glad, we are proud” – what Srila Prabhupad did is obvious, but to appreciate Sridhar Maharaj requires some extra sukriti; “Bhaktivinod Thakur is sitting in front of me and I need to go to London and interview his daughter?” – we are not interested in any flesh connection;

Srila Madhusudan Maharaja

The purpose of coming to parikrama is to hear hari-katha;
Pulling the rug to let the speaker know, it’s time to wrap it up; Tirtha Maharaja’s “death march”; the merciful Nityananda curses Shivananda Sen’s family for the prasadam being late; boatman Sandeep didn’t want to take the parikrama dog across the river; a dog that got a plate of prasadam after parikrama.
