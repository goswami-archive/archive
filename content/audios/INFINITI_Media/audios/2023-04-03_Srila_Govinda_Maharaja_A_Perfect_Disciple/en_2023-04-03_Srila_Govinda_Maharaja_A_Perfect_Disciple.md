Apr 02, 2023. Disappearance Festival of Srila Govinda Maharaja
— Summary: “Sri Guru and His Grace”: a guide on guru-disciple relationships; the logo of Sri Chaitanya Saraswat Math: we are Guru-centric; Mahaprabhu and Ramananda Ray: a sannyasi is taking instructions from a grihastha shudra; separation from the Vaishnava is the most unbearable suffering; who is the highest devotee; without Radharani Krishna is not so beautiful; Das Goswami’s prayers; a perfect disciple; “He did not breathe in or out without saying, 'Jay Guru Maharaj!'”;

Sri Rupa is the leader of all juniors; “Dignity of the Divine Servitor” and “The Affectionate Guidance”; the dignified divine servitor; Guru Maharaja’s servitor; bathing with the Chaitanya-ashtakam of Rupa Goswami; devotion that must be praised;

Govinda Maharaja’s speech at the opening of Chandrodaya Mandir; “The holy Trinity”; a Vaishnav is jolly; you can get Sridhar Maharaja’s susiddhanta by reading his books, with Govinda Maharaja it’s about personal dealings; Govinda Maharaja’s “secret weapon”; “he is the cure”; a moment of sadhu-sanga gives all perfections; Vishwamitra and Vasishtha Muni; gratitude.
